Statutory Compliance & Certifications

Committed to transparency, safety, and operational excellence across all facets of our business.

Income Tax Compliance

Tableworth Hospitality Private Limited is fully compliant with all Income Tax regulations as prescribed by the Government of India. The company ensures timely filing of returns, accurate financial reporting, and adherence to all statutory provisions, reflecting our commitment to financial transparency and accountability.

GST Certification

We are a registered entity under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) framework. Tableworth Hospitality ensures proper invoicing, timely GST return filings, and strict compliance with tax regulations, enabling seamless and transparent business transactions with our clients and partners.

Professional Tax

The company complies with all Professional Tax requirements applicable in the state. We ensure timely deductions and payments on behalf of employees, maintaining full adherence to state taxation norms.

Provident Fund (PF)

Tableworth Hospitality is registered under the Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF) scheme. We ensure timely contributions towards employee provident funds, supporting long-term financial security and employee welfare.

ESIC Compliance

We are compliant with the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) regulations, providing medical and financial benefits to eligible employees. This ensures enhanced social security and well-being for our workforce.

Work Compensation

The company adheres to all Workmen Compensation regulations, ensuring that employees are protected against workplace-related injuries or incidents. This reflects our commitment to employee safety and responsible workplace practices.

Labour Licence

Tableworth Hospitality holds a valid Labour Licence as per applicable laws. We strictly follow labour regulations, ensuring ethical employment practices, fair wages, and a safe working environment.

Staff Group Insurance

We provide Staff Group Insurance coverage to our employees, offering financial protection and security. This initiative highlights our focus on employee welfare and risk management.

FSSAI Compliance

The company is fully compliant with Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) regulations. We maintain the highest standards of food safety, hygiene, and quality across all our operations.

ISO 22000:2018 Certification

Tableworth Hospitality is certified under ISO 22000:2018 for Food Safety Management Systems. This certification demonstrates our commitment to maintaining international standards in food safety, ensuring safe and high-quality catering services.

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

We are certified under ISO 9001:2015 for Quality Management Systems. This reflects our dedication to consistent service quality, continuous improvement, and customer satisfaction across all operations.
