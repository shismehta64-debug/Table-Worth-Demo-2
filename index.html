WHO WE ARE

India's Most Trusted
Hospitality Partner

Table Worth Hospitality Private Limited is one of India's most trusted names in outsourced catering and hospitality support services. With over 30 years of proven excellence under the leadership of Director Manoj Rai, we've built our reputation on a foundation of consistent quality, passionate people, and an unwavering commitment to every client we serve.

Headquartered in Surat, Gujarat, our experienced team of professionals spans food production, service, administration, facility management, and manpower deployment. From hospital canteens to corporate cafeterias and industrial dining halls — we bring the same meticulous care and warmth to every table we set.

We hold ISO 22000:2018 (Food Safety Management) and ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management) certifications, and maintain full statutory compliance including FSSAI, GST, Provident Fund, ESIC, and Labour Licences. We don't just meet standards — we set them.
